# 📊 Swiggy Restaurant Performance Analysis

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes restaurant-level data to understand performance, customer engagement, and city-level trends similar to a food delivery platform like Swiggy.

The goal is to extract meaningful insights that can help improve visibility, customer experience, and operational efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Identify top-performing restaurants based on ratings and popularity  
- Compare city-level performance  
- Analyze delivery efficiency  
- Discover patterns in customer engagement  

---

## 📁 Dataset
The dataset contains restaurant-level information including:

- City and Area  
- Restaurant Name  
- Average Ratings  
- Total Ratings (Popularity)  
- Price  
- Delivery Time  
- Food Type  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Python  
- Pandas  
- Matplotlib  
- Jupyter Notebook  

---

## 📊 Key Analysis

### 🔹 Top Restaurants
- Identified restaurants with high ratings and strong popularity  

### 🔹 Most Popular Restaurants
- Ranked restaurants based on total ratings  

### 🔹 City Performance
- Compared cities based on average ratings and engagement  

### 🔹 Delivery Time Analysis
- Evaluated delivery efficiency across cities  

### 🔹 High-Quality Restaurants
- Identified cities with the highest number of top-rated restaurants  

---

## 📈 Visualizations
- Average ratings by city  
- Top restaurants by popularity  
- Delivery time comparison across cities  

---

## 💡 Key Insights

1. High-rated restaurants are often under-discovered compared to highly popular ones  
2. Some cities outperform others in both quality and customer engagement  
3. Delivery time varies significantly across cities and impacts user experience  

---

## 🚀 Recommendations

- Promote high-rated but low-visibility restaurants to increase engagement  
- Improve delivery efficiency in slower cities  
- Focus business expansion in high-performing cities  

---

## 📌 Conclusion
This analysis demonstrates how data can be used to drive business decisions in a food delivery platform by identifying opportunities for growth, optimization, and improved user experience.
